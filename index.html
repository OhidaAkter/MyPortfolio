Ohida Akter


My research is driven by a passion for Artificial Intelligence and its potential to solve complex real-world problems. I am particularly focused on the intersection of Security and Healthcare, aiming to build systems that are not only intelligent but also robust and beneficial to human well-being. Beyond my core research, I am deeply committed to the Education sector, where I hope to contribute by making advanced technology more accessible and understandable for the next generation of learners.
During my downtime, I love building websites and mobile apps. I enjoy the challenge of creating designs that are easy for people to use. My real strength is my curiosity—if I find something difficult, I start to learn those difficult topics to know why they are difficult to me. I also love to learn new languages, whether it’s the logic of a programming language like Forth or the melody of Spanish.
